Hi, I'm Rossi!

Until recently, in addition to my responsibilities at NoPo Ventures, I also led a team of expert designers, developers, and marketers as CMO and director of business development at Artesian Future Technology--one of the most challenging and rewarding experiences of my career.

I've had the great pleasure of working with numerous brands over the years including NVIDIA, Volta Computers, Aurora Provisions, Bixby Co, and Northern Girl, to name just a few.

I'm passionate about the intersection of business, technology, and creativity. I thrive by finding ways to align my sense of purpose with what I do everyday. I get to use color, typography, digital interaction, and visual language to translate and communicate ideas into tangible results for partners and clients--and for that I count myself as truly blessed!

I studied at the USM School of Business and the Art Academy of Midcoast Maine, but I learned about life from sailing around the world with my parents as a child, and about technology from being of the first generation of people raised on the internet.
