Hi, I'm Rossi!

I work with an array of web technologies at NoPo Ventures to create stunning websites and exciting digital experiences. Before that I led a team of expert marketers and product developers as CMO and director of business development at Artesian Future Technology in San Francisco.

I've had the great pleasure of working with a number of fantastic brands over the years including NVIDIA, Volta Computers, Sysco, Coastal Farms & Foods, Aurora Provisions, Bixby Co, and Northern Girl, among many others.

I'm passionate about the intersection of business, technology, and creativity. I love that I get to use color, typography, digital interaction, and visual language to translate and communicate ideas into tangible results for partners and clients--for that I count myself as truly blessed!
